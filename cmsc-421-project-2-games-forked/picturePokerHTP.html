How To Play: Picture Poker

Match Cards & Combos Together to Beat Your Opponent!

Each Round You Will Be Given a Hand of Five Cards.

You May Hold or Draw Any Cards from Your Hand.

If You Win the Round, You Will Be Awarded Coins Based on the Combo You Used.

First to 20 Coins Wins!
